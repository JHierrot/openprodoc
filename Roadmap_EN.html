   OpenProdoc: Document Management System            Castellano 

The defined evolution for OpenProdoc is:

Version 1.2 (Autumn 2015)

  • RIS standard format support for document references.
  • Creation of personalized reports and export formats (html, XML, csv, txt)
  • Documents and metadata Full Text search.

Version 1.3 (Summer 2016)

  • Automatic classification (assignment of document type) based on document content and previous training with examples.
  • Document Scanning directly from OpenProdoc

This is the expected roadmap, but dates and/or functionality can change, depending on requirements by users.
