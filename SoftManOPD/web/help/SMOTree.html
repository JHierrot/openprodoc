SMO Forms Tree

Software Management OpenProdoc (SMO) tree of operations and forms.

  • Products: Displays the list of products internal and external (according to the filters selected and the permissions of the current user).
    • Update: Displays a form for updating the selected product.
    • Versions: Displays the list of versions of the selected product.
      • Add: Displays a form for adding a new version of the current product.
      • Copy: Copies the current version and displays a form with the fields so they can be modified before saving.
      • Update: Displays a form for updating the selected version of the product.
      • Delete: Displays a form for confirmation before deleting the selected product version and ALL its issues, folders and documents.
      • List Documents: Displays a form for maintenance of all the documents and folders structure under the selected product version.
      • List Issues: Displays a form for maintenance of issues of the current product version.
        • Add: Displays a form for adding a new issue of the current product version.
        • Update: Displays a form for updating the selected issue.
        • Delete: Displays a form for confirmation before deleting the selected issue and ALL its folders and documents.
      • Manage Dependencies: Displays a form for maintenance of dependencies of the selected version.
        • Add: Displays a form for adding a new dependencie of the current product version.
        • Update: Displays a form for updating the selected dependencie.
        • Delete: Displays a form for confirmation before deleting the selected dependencie.
      • Dependencies Tree: Displays a tree of ALL the products this version depends on and the dependencies of them.
      • Impact Tree: Display a tree of all the products in which this product version is used.
    • Delete: Displays a form for confirmation before deleting the selected product and ALL its versions, folders and documents.
    • List Documents: Displays a form for maintenance of all the documents and folders structure under the selected product.
  • Departments: Shows the list of departments of the company (according to the filters selected and the permissions of the current user).
    • Add: Displays a form for adding a department.
    • Update: Displays a form for updating the selected department.
    • Delete: Displays a form for confirmation before deleting the selected department and ALL its products, versions, folders and documents.
    • Add Product: Displays a form for adding a product to the selected department.
    • List Documents: Displays a form for maintenance of all the documents and folders structure under the selected department.
  • Issues: Shows the list of issues/problems of the internal and external products (according to the filters selected and the permissions of the current user).
    • Add: Displays a form for adding a new issue to a product.
    • Update: Displays a form for updating the selected issue.
    • Delete: Displays a form for confirmation before deleting the selected issue and ALL its folders and documents.
    • List Documents: Displays a form for maintenance of all the documents and folders structure under the selected department.
  • Soft Companies: Shows the list of software companies or providers of products (according to the filters selected and the permissions of the current user).
    • Add: Displays a form for adding a Software Company.
    • Update: Displays a form for updating the selected Software Company.
    • Delete: Displays a form for confirmation before deleting the selected Software Company and ALL its products, versions, folders and documents.
    • Add Product: Displays a form for adding a product to the selected Software Company.
    • List Documents: Displays a form for maintenance of all the documents and folders structure under the selected Software Company.
  • Search: Shows a form for searching documents of any kind and in any level of the structure of SMO (according to the permissions of the current user).
  • Help: Shows the help pages.
  • About: Shows a form with information of the versions of SMO and OpenProdoc.
  • Exit: Closes the session in SMO.
