Help SMO Software Companies Add

This form allows to add a new Software Company. All the fields follow the interface conventions of OpenProdoc:

  • Standard fields: Without special mark
  • Required fields: With a character "*"
  • Thesaurus controlled: With a button with character "T" for selecting and "-" for deleting
  • Multivalued: With a button with character "*"
  • Multivalued and Thesaurus controlled: With a button with characters "T*"

With the default configuration, the fields to fill are:

  • Title/Name:Name of Software Company.
  • Description:Description of Software Company
  • ContactContact of Company
  • Mail of contactMail of contact of Company
  • Phone of contactPhone of contact of Company
  • Url:Url of Company

This operation will create a new folder of the type configured (the default configured type is SoftProvider) that could contain all the subfolders and documents specific for this company

In SMO Tree there is a complete view of operations and forms.
